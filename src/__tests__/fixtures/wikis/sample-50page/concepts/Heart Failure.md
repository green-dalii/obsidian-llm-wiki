## Definition

Heart Failure (HF) is a clinical syndrome in which the heart cannot pump sufficiently to meet the body's metabolic demands, or can only do so at elevated filling pressures. In the synthetic Pinewood Heart Center cohort, HF is the most common cause of 30-day readmission per [[sources/Quality Dashboard]].

## Classification

- HF with reduced ejection fraction (HFrEF)
- HF with preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF)
