## Definition

Antiplatelet Therapy refers to pharmacologic agents that inhibit platelet aggregation, used in the synthetic Pinewood Heart Center for primary and secondary prevention of atherothrombotic events. The dominant clinical context is post-percutaneous coronary intervention (see [[entities/Stent Implant]]).

## Agents

- Aspirin (irreversible COX-1 inhibitor)
- P2Y12 inhibitors (clopidogrel, ticagrelor, prasugrel)
