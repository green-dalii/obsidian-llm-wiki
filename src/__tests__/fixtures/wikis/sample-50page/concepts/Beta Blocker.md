## Definition

Beta Blockers are a class of medications that block beta-adrenergic receptors, used as guideline-directed therapy in [[concepts/Heart Failure]] with reduced ejection fraction, in post-[[concepts/Myocardial Infarction]] care, and in [[concepts/Hypertension]].
