## Definition

Myocardial Infarction (MI) is the death of cardiac muscle due to prolonged ischemia, most commonly caused by acute thrombotic occlusion of a coronary artery in the setting of underlying [[concepts/Coronary Artery Disease]]. It is the dominant acute event category in the synthetic Pinewood Heart Center cohort.

## Classification

- STEMI (ST-elevation MI): transmural infarction
- NSTEMI (non-ST-elevation MI): subendocardial infarction

## Management

Door-to-balloon time is tracked per [[sources/Quality Dashboard]]. Post-event management includes [[concepts/Antiplatelet Therapy]] and [[concepts/Statin Pharmacology]].
